Student Life

Weekly once classes (Offline & Online options available.

Our 1-year Montessori Teacher Training course is designed exclusively for women, with no age limit. Students with 10th, 12th (pass or fail), or any degree can enroll. Classes are held once a week, with both online and offline options. Graduates receive a CBSE Board Certification, and scholarships are available for BC, MBC, SC, and ST candidates
